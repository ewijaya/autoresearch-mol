\subsection{Hypothesis Summary}
\label{sec:hypothesis_summary}

Table~\ref{tab:hypotheses} summarizes the four pre-registered hypotheses, their predictions, statistical tests, and outcomes.

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\caption{Summary of pre-registered hypotheses. All $p$-values are Holm--Bonferroni adjusted within their respective families.}
\label{tab:hypotheses}
\small
\begin{tabular}{@{}llp{3.8cm}lp{3.2cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Name} & \textbf{Prediction} & \textbf{Test} & \textbf{Outcome} \\
\midrule
H1 & Architecture clustering & Agent discovers different architectures per domain & Permutation test on Gower distance & \textbf{Supported} ($p = 0.004$) \\
\midrule
H2 & Domain knowledge rediscovery & Agent rediscovers known molecular techniques & Code diff classification vs.\ 5 known techniques & \textbf{Supported}: 4/5 techniques matched \\
\midrule
H3 & Transfer universality & Domain-specific architectures degrade on cross-domain transfer & Cross-domain transfer + 4 sub-tests & \textbf{Not supported}: all 41/41 innovations universal ($<$1\% degradation) \\
\quad H3a & \quad Asymmetric transfer & Transfer degrades asymmetrically & Pairwise degradation & Not supported \\
\quad H3b & \quad Layer specificity & Late layers are domain-specific & Layer freezing & Partially supported \\
\quad H3c & \quad Length matching & Sequence length is an artifact & Length truncation & Not supported \\
\quad H3d & \quad Innovation classification & 35\% of innovations are universal & Binomial test & Contradicted ($p = 2 \times 10^{-19}$; 100\% universal) \\
\midrule
H4 & Architecture vs.\ HP decomposition & Architecture search adds value beyond HP tuning & Bootstrap CI + Welch's $t$ on AUC-OC & \textbf{Domain-dependent}: 81\% for NLP ($p = 0.009$); $-$51\% for SMILES ($p = 0.001$) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Agent Prompts}

The full agent system prompt (\texttt{program.md}) and HP-only prompt (\texttt{program\_hponly.md}) are included in the released code repository. The key difference: the HP-only prompt adds the constraint ``Do NOT change model architecture: no new layers, no attention pattern changes, no activation function changes, no model structure changes.''
